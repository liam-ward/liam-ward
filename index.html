Hi, I'm Liam Ward.

I'm an engineer, an artist, and an explorer.

Engineering

In May 2022 I graduated from Boston University with a B.S. in Mechanical Engineering, concentration in Aerospace Engineering. In Fall 2022 I will begin my studies at Purdue University, pursuing a M.S. in Aeronautics & Astronautics.
Through industry internships at ABL Space Systems, Inversion Space, and Hedron Space, I have learned to operate and execute in the fast-paced commercial space environment.
By my participation in research at Boston University and future work at Purdue University, I have gained and will continue to gain first hand experience in academic inquiry, applying the scientific method to solving unsolved problems while appropriately documenting and communicating my work.

Artistry

I've been playing piano since I was six, but in many ways the music in me is much older than that, coming to me from generations of my Irish family. Starting at age 11, I have regularly accompanied Catholic church services in the Philadelphia and Boston areas on piano and organ. I have experience accompanying individual vocalists as well as youth and adult choirs.
During my time at La Salle College High School, I played piano in our award-winning competitive jazz band.
In my free time I also play traditional Irish music on the button-keyed accordion and recently I picked up the ukulele!

Exploration

I love to explore new places, new ideas, and new opportunities. I am constantly seeking new perspectives to transform how I see the world around me. I hope that through my exploration, I am able to have a positive impact on others, transform the world for the better, and arrive back at where I began, knowing the place for the first time.
